## Summary

Polish for **mobile touch** and **lesson navigation**: entire lesson rows are tappable (guest and signed-in), practice cards reset to the front when advancing, and signed-in practice uses the same tap-to-reveal touch handling as guest browse while keeping **1–5 ratings** and the practice scheduler.

---

## Changed

- **Lesson rows** — One tap target for title and subtitle; title editor still works for deck owners without navigating away
- **Signed-in practice touch** — Center tap to reveal; card resets to front after rating advances to the next card
- **Guest swipe practice** — Horizontal swipe thresholds, edge tap zones, and invisible prev/next hit areas for more reliable navigation

## Fixed

- **Sign out** — Redirects to Explore instead of the login page
