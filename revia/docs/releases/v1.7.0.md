## Summary

Guest practice cards now feel like a **physical deck you drag**: the card follows your finger, rotates slightly, and flies off when you commit a swipe. Navigation **loops endlessly** through the lesson.

---

## Added

- **Drag-to-swipe cards** — Card tracks your finger with rotation and fade, like a dating-app card stack
- **Peek preview** — Next/previous card visible behind while dragging
- **Endless loop** — Last card wraps to first (and vice versa) in guest swipe practice

## Changed

- **Swipe direction** — Drag left for next, drag right for previous (matches natural card-stack behavior)
- **Snap-back** — Release before the threshold and the card springs back to center

## Unchanged

- **Signed-in practice** — Still uses 1–5 ratings and the practice scheduler
- **Daily Review** — Unchanged
