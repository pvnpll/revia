## Summary

Revia now has **two separate learning modes**: **Practice** (primary, endless adaptive sessions) and **Daily Review** (secondary, date-based spaced repetition). Opening the app lands you directly on a practice card. Deck and lesson flows use the same shared card viewer with independent scheduling logic.

---

## Added

- **Practice mode** ‚Äî Endless adaptive review; cards re-enter the queue by rating (1‚Äì5), not by due date
- **`PracticeScheduler`** ‚Äî Pure client-side queue logic with randomized gaps per rating (`src/lib/scheduler/practice-scheduler.ts`)
- **`DailyReviewScheduler`** ‚Äî Wrapper for existing SRS used only by Daily Review (`src/lib/scheduler/daily-review-scheduler.ts`)
- **`GET /api/practice/cards`** ‚Äî Fetch practice cards by deck, lesson, or recent decks
- **`PracticeSession`** component ‚Äî Shared fullscreen viewer for practice (used on `/practice`, deck detail, lessons)
- **Recent-deck practice pool** ‚Äî Default practice uses cards from your 5 most recently opened decks (all cards per deck, any lesson)
- **Deck access tracking** ‚Äî Opening a deck or starting practice updates recency for the default pool
- **Distinct rating colors** ‚Äî Minimal border tints for ratings 1‚Äì5 (red, violet, yellow, sky, emerald)
- **Unit tests** ‚Äî `practice-scheduler.test.ts`

## Changed

- **App entry** ‚Äî Login and `/` redirect to `/practice` with a card loaded immediately
- **Navigation order** ‚Äî Dashboard ‚Üí Practice ‚Üí Decks ‚Üí Explore
- **Daily Review** ‚Äî Remains at `/review`; secondary CTA on dashboard (no longer the primary tab)
- **Lessons** ‚Äî Tap a lesson opens Practice (lesson-scoped only); no longer updates SRS scheduling during lesson study
- **Deck practice** ‚Äî ‚ÄúPractice deck‚Äù includes all non-suspended cards in the deck, regardless of lesson
- **Imported decks** ‚Äî Visibility toggle hidden; only original authors control public sharing (API enforced)
- **Explore** ‚Äî Public decks visible to everyone, including the author who published them
- **Settings copy** ‚Äî Explore descriptions updated for inclusive public deck listing

## Fixed

- **Practice session init loop** ‚Äî Resolved ‚ÄúMaximum update depth exceeded‚Äù when loading practice cards

## Architecture notes

| Mode | Scheduler | Persists SRS? | Queue behavior |
|------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **Practice** | `PracticeScheduler` | No | Endless; rating ‚Üí reinsert after N cards |
| **Daily Review** | `DailyReviewScheduler` | Yes | Due cards only; finishes when caught up |

Both modes share `StudyCardViewer` and the same 1‚Äì5 rating UI.
